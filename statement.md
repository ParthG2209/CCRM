# Statement

## Project Title

Campus Course & Records Manager (CCRM)

## Problem Statement

College departments need a lightweight system to manage student records, course offerings, enrollments and academic performance without the overhead of a full LMS.

## Scope

The system supports basic student management, course and offering management, enrollment with simple validation rules, grade entry, and transcript-style reports in a console interface.

## Target Users

- Department coordinators
- Faculty handling courses
- Academic office staff

## High-level Features

- Student CRUD operations
- Course and offering CRUD operations
- Enrollment and withdrawal with basic checks
- Grade recording and GPA calculation
- Simple console-based reporting
